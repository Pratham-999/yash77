About Us

Arts, Commerce and Science College, Palus, a leading higher education institute, has been based in Sangli, Maharashtra since 1992. Institute offers 10 courses which are Degree and Diploma in Full Time mode. Overall, Institute has diversified 7 UG courses. It is a partnership institute which is accredited with Grade B+ by NAAC. Institute enjoys a good reputation for courses like PGDCA, M.A., M.Sc., B.A., B.Com, B.Sc., BBA, BCA in the streams of IT & Software, Humanities & Social Sciences, Science, Accounting & Commerce and Business & Management Studies Students can opt courses from these. Over the period, Arts, Commerce and Science College, Palus has gained expertize through its trained and experienced faculty in the fields of English, Computer Science. Institute has a well supported campus with facilities like Auditorium, Cafeteria, Girls Hostel, Gym, Hospital / Medical Facilities, Hostel, Labs, Library, Sports Complex. The Institute has 2 hostels facility with capacity of 60 students
